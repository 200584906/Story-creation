Instructions to Use Toy Story Creation Web App:

  1. Select Story Elements:
    • Click the numbered buttons (1 to 5) on the left to choose the following elements for your story:
    • Button 1: Generates a Noun (e.g., "The turkey").
    • Button 2: Generates a Verb (e.g., "sat on").
    • Button 3: Generates an Adjective (e.g., "a funny").
    • Button 4: Generates a second Noun (e.g., "goat").
    • Button 5: Generates a Place (e.g., "on the moon").
  2. Create a Random Story: Press the Random Story button to generate an entire random story in one click.
  3. View Your Story: The story you create will be displayed in the large text area in the center of the page.
  4. Speak the Story: Press the Speak button to hear your story read aloud.
  5. Reset Your Story: Press the Reset button to clear all the selected elements and start fresh.
  6. Have Fun! Combine different words to create fun, silly, or creative stories.
